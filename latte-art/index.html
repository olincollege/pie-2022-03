Meet the BaristaBot.

The BaristaBot is a Latte Art making machine that can produce beautiful designs on your latte with just the click of a button! As a PIE team it was important for us to choose a project that was not only equally balanced electrically, mechanically, and in software, but would provide each of us an interesting challenge that would maintain our engagement and enthusiasm with the project. And so through our collective love of lattes, art, and challenging tasks, the idea of the BaristaBot came to fruition.
