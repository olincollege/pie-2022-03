Electrical Systems

A description of our electrical design

Circuit Diagram

Our electrical design was quite simple and didnâ€™t require any analysis during its construction. At its core is an Arduino Uno R3, with an Adafruit Motorshield V2.3 attached. The Motorshield is connected to an external 12 V power supply and drives our X and Y stepper motors. The pins of the Arduino are used to collect readings from our X and Y limit switches during homing, and they also drive our servo. The servo controls our pen switching mechanism.
