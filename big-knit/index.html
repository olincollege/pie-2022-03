Welcome to Big Knit
an automated, sustainable knitting machine.

Big Knit is a knitting mechanism that is hooked up to an Arduino. Via linear motion, the machine knits a knit piece of user-specified width and height out of t-shirt yarn. This project was made with sustainability in mind. Sustainability drove our manufacturing decisions and is reflected in the materials used. Learn more about how this lens impacted our materials on the Bill of Materials page.
