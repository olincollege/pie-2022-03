MEET THE BOT

Mehndi Didi is a mehndi (henna) applicator robot built to automate the demanding process of applying detailed mehndi design, which usually takes up to a few hours depending on the intricacy of the chosen design. Mehndi Didi main features include an XY Gantry sytem controlled by G-code pathing. This is a work-in progess, the team's detailed progress in mechanical, electrical/ firmware, and software can be seen here and also in this project log.
