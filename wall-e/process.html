Reflection

We had to overcome a lot of obstacles during this project from broken bones to COVID. However, through those ups and downs we were still able to create a project that we were proud of. That in itself is a huge accomplishment, not to mention the fact that we had a team member who lives further away from the rest of us. Making the most of class time and going above and beyond to communicate as a team was why we worked so well as a team. In conclusion we had great teamwork and with great teamwork we created an amazing product.
