Overview for electronic integration.

Intelligent, speedy and powerful, electronic part is the heart of WALL-E. We equipped Wall-e with two powerful dual-core esp32 processors as well as an arduino for body movement control. All movements of WALL-E could be controlled by virtual controller integrated in user's smart phone application, you may drive it around the building and gain high quility real-time video streaming send back from WALL-E remotely. For a better vision, you may also adjust horizontal angle of WALL-E's neck by smart phone controller.

WIFI live streaming eyes with face recognition.

WELL-E's eyes are implemented based ESP32-CAM module. Program inside is developed based on the code for a web server. Once you logged into the IP for ESP32-CAM, you could adjust parameters and even enroll human's faces.
